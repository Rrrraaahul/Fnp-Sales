# Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Sales Analysis Dashboard** designed to analyze sales performance across multiple dimensions such as time, occasion, city, product, and category. The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly understand business trends, customer behavior, and revenue drivers for data-driven decision-making.

The dashboard is built to provide both high-level KPIs and detailed drill-down insights, making it suitable for management reviews and analytical reporting.

---

## Key Objectives

* Monitor overall sales performance and revenue trends
* Identify high-performing occasions, products, and categories
* Analyze order distribution across cities and time periods
* Evaluate customer spending behavior and delivery efficiency

---

## Key Metrics (KPIs)

* **Total Orders:** 1,000
* **Total Revenue:** ₹35,20,984
* **Average Delivery Time:** 5.53 days
* **Average Customer Spend:** ₹3,520.98

These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of operational efficiency and business health.

---

## Dashboard Insights

### 1. Orders Trend Analysis

* Order volume fluctuates throughout the day
* Peak ordering activity is observed during evening hours
* Helps identify optimal time windows for promotions and campaigns

### 2. Occasion-wise Sales Analysis

* Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan contribute the highest revenue share
* Valentine’s Day and Holi also show strong sales performance
* Demonstrates the importance of occasion-based demand

### 3. Month-wise Revenue Trend

* Revenue peaks during February, August, and October
* Mid-year months show relatively lower revenue
* Reflects seasonal and festive sales patterns

### 4. City-wise Order Distribution

* Top cities by order volume include Imphal, Dhanbad, and Jorhat
* Orders are well distributed across multiple cities
* Indicates balanced geographic market penetration

### 5. Product-wise Revenue Contribution

* Magnam Set and Qila Gift are top revenue-generating products
* Other products contribute steady and consistent revenue
* Highlights key products for inventory and marketing focus

### 6. Category-wise Revenue Analysis

* Colors category generates the highest revenue
* Cake and Raksha Bandhan categories are strong contributors
* Remaining categories add diversification to revenue streams
